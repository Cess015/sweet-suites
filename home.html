Short Journey

We are high school best-friends who love to taste
various desserts. Enojoying travels and taste unique desserts
along the way. Knowing that many desserts are unique and people
might not be informed about them.

Experiencing the taste and aroma of a lovely desserts truly
made our day. Remembering the laughs and the hopes of bringing
those memories back in a single taste like non other,
living in heavens of sweetness.

This blog will also serve as a remembrance of all the
special desserts that
we have tasted and the moments we spent together as friends.

We stopped traveling as we all became busy with our own lives.
We are still friends, but rarely together by now unlike before.
We decided to leave our memories but ofcourse
it is still in our hearts

This blog is a remembrance for us and a new memory for you. We hope you
will enjoy learning new things through this blog.

Enjoying eating these delicious desserts as much as we did.
Giving you a new start through our recommendations.

We hope that through this blog, you
will also meet people that has a same taste as yours.
